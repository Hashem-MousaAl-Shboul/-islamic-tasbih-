# Reduce spacing on Settings & Statistics screens

**What will change:**

- Reduce the large bottom padding in both the Settings and Statistics scroll areas (currently oversized, causing unnecessary empty space below content)
- Remove the extra 100px spacer at the bottom of both screens — it's redundant with the existing bottom padding
- Tighten vertical gaps between cards and sections on both screens for a more compact, mobile-friendly layout
- Slightly reduce header padding for a more compact top area
- Reduce margins between section titles and cards on both screens

**Result:**
Both the Settings and Statistics screens will feel tighter and more natural on Android and iOS, with no large empty gaps between or below content sections.
