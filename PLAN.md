# Translate Privacy Policy & Terms of Use pages based on selected language

**What changes**

Currently, the Privacy Policy and Terms of Use screens show all content in English regardless of the selected language. This update will make all text on those screens translate automatically when you switch languages in Settings.

**Features**
- Privacy Policy page will display fully in the selected language (all headings, paragraphs, and labels)
- Terms of Use page will display fully in the selected language
- The "Last Updated" date label will also be translated
- All 9 supported languages will be covered: Arabic, English, Bengali, Spanish, French, Indonesian, Malay, Turkish, Urdu

**What stays the same**
- The Settings screen layout and "About" section (Privacy Policy, Terms of Use, Version) remain unchanged
- The section title "About" already translates correctly
- Navigation and back buttons work the same way

**Scope of changes**
- Add ~30 new translation entries for Privacy Policy content across all 9 languages
- Add ~25 new translation entries for Terms of Use content across all 9 languages
- Update both the Privacy Policy and Terms of Use screens to use translated text instead of hardcoded English
